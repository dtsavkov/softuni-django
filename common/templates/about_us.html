ABOUT US


History

SM PLUS Electronics Ltd. was established in 2000 year in the city of Plovdiv, Bulgaria with main orientation in the following directions - SMD and PTH modules and montage, design and manufacture of hybrid integrated circuits, design and manufacture of single and double-sided PCBs, design and manufacturing of control and automation components and devices and distribution of electronic components.
 
One of the most important aspects of our company's activity is the continual improvement of our products, not only in the functional side but also their meteorological characteristics. Our purpose is to satisfy our customers' requirements and also to answer the high European standards and regulations.
